Q-Sweeper

Project Website ➹

Honourable Mention at The New School X IBM Quantum Design Jam | Fall 2021

Link➹

Co-creators: Apurv Rayate, Hyacinth Weng, Tanvi Mishra, Yiping Dong

We built Q-sweeper as a part of the Quantum Design Jam hosted by The New School in partnership with the IBM Quantum team. Q-sweeper stands for quantum minesweeper. The original game is redesigned by applying quantum concepts (noise and entanglement) and visualising data from a quantum algorithm to enhance the player's understanding of quantum mechanics. Visit the website for an in-depth explanation and further documentation..
