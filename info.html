About

Hello! I’m Shangshang Li [she/her], a designer and creative technologist with a passion for crafting interactive experiences that merge art, technology, and human connection. With a background in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design, I specialize in creating installations, games, websites, and applications that bring ideas to life in engaging, innovative ways.

My work often focuses on exploring human-AI interaction, immersive design, and the ways digital tools can be used to create social impact. Each project is an opportunity to shape technology to be more intuitive, meaningful, and accessible, sparking curiosity and enriching the everyday lives of users.

As I advance my studies in Information Science, I’m dedicated to expanding my expertise in data visualization, interactive media, and speculative design. I’m inspired by the potential of technology to bridge gaps, tell stories, and make complex concepts approachable. Through my work, I strive to combine artistic expression with technical precision, all while pushing the boundaries of user-centered design.

I’m also exploring product management, where I’m excited to bring together data-driven insights, AI innovation, and a commitment to user-focused design to create meaningful, scalable solutions. Based in Ithca, NY, I’m thrilled to share my journey with you.
